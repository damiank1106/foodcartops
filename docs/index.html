Privacy Policy — Megan Yhung Food Cart

Last updated: December 24, 2025

App: Megan Yhung Food Cart (app.rork.foodcartops)

This Privacy Policy describes how Megan Young Food Cart Co. (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the Megan Young Food Cart Co. mobile application (“App”).

Summary: Megan Young Food Cart Co. is designed to store your operational data (sales, expenses, products, shifts, notes, and optional images like receipt photos) locally on your device. The App does not sell your data.

Information the App Processes

The App may process the following types of information that you enter or choose to add:

  • Account/Work data: usernames, PIN/password (used for local sign-in), roles, and settings.
  • Business records: sales totals, items, expenses, shift records, notes, and related operational details.
  • Images you choose: receipt photos, profile images, and/or product images you attach in the App.
  • Exports you initiate: CSV/PDF files generated by the App when you choose to export or share data.

What We Collect Automatically

We do not collect personal information automatically through the App (no advertising identifiers, no third-party analytics SDKs for tracking). Any data you create is intended to remain on your device unless you export/share it.

Device Permissions Used

The App may request access to:

  • Camera — to let you take photos (for example, receipt images) that you choose to attach.
  • Photos/Media (Storage) — to let you pick images from your library and to save/share exported files you generate.
  • Internet — required by the operating system for some features (e.g., opening links or system sharing flows). The App is not intended to upload your stored records to our servers.
  • Biometrics (optional) — if enabled on your device, the App may use Face ID/biometrics for local unlock where supported.

You can control permissions in your device settings. If you deny a permission, some features (like attaching a receipt photo) may not work.

How Your Data Is Stored

FoodCartOps stores App data locally on your device (for example in a local database). Images you attach may be stored as local files referenced by the App. Export files (CSV/PDF) are generated on your device when you request them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. The App only shares data when you choose to do so, for example:

  • When you email, print, or share an exported CSV/PDF using your device’s share options.
  • When you choose to attach images from your library or take a photo, your device provider may handle that action (camera/photos).

If you share an export via email or another service, the privacy practices of that service will apply to the shared file.

Third-Party Services

The App uses system services and libraries provided by your mobile platform (Android/iOS) to support features like camera access, file handling, printing, email composition, and sharing. We do not operate a server that your records are uploaded to by default.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • On-device data: You can delete records inside the App (where features are provided) and/or remove the App to delete locally stored data from your device.
  • Exports: Exported files you create may remain in your device storage or the target app you shared them with, until you delete them.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored within the App (for example, using platform security features and secure local storage for sensitive values). No method of storage is 100% secure, but we aim to minimize risk.

Children’s Privacy

FoodCartOps is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for business/operational use. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
